Some works

Project Noah

Project Noah can be best described as a set of algorithms aimed to predict the areas that would be most affected by an incoming disaster (like floods) and to prepare optimal evacuation strategies for the people living in those areas. The process involves the use of remotely sensed data and satellite imagery belonging to different regions of the electromagnetic spectrum. The data and images thus obtained provide us with the input values on which the algorithms involved in the project are based upon.
